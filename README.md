# Zentra Project: Shannon's Food Truck Digital Platform

## 📌 Project Overview
[cite_start]This project involves developing a digital ordering and management platform for Shannon’s fast-food truck business[cite: 17]. [cite_start]The business specializes in local comfort food like amagwinya, beverages, and Kotas, typically serving customers with a quick 10 to 15-minute turnaround[cite: 17]. 

[cite_start]As the business scales its operations from two to three active trucks, this digital solution aims to accelerate growth, enhance brand exposure, and cultivate a stronger community presence, particularly in the Parklands area[cite: 17, 21].

## 🎯 Objectives
* [cite_start]Develop a responsive website or dedicated mobile application based on final client preference[cite: 18].
* [cite_start]Enable customers to seamlessly browse the menu, place orders online, and book services[cite: 18].
* [cite_start]Transition to premium service tiers on platforms like Supabase or Firebase as the user base expands[cite: 20].

## 📅 Project Roadmap
[cite_start]The project is structured into four primary phases spanning 17 weeks[cite: 22]:
1. [cite_start]**Phase 1: Initiation** - Project kickoff, requirements gathering, WBS planning, risk management, and economic feasibility studies[cite: 22].
2. [cite_start]**Phase 2: Design** - UI design for the website and the underlying ordering system[cite: 22].
3. [cite_start]**Phase 3: Development** - Core system development and live inventory integration[cite: 22].
4. [cite_start]**Phase 4: Testing & Delivery** - System testing, bug fixes, client review, and final handover[cite: 22].

## ⚠️ Current Development Status & Constraints
* [cite_start]**Emulator Performance:** The team is currently experiencing performance bottlenecks when running Android Virtual Devices within Android Studio[cite: 52]. [cite_start]To mitigate this, development testing is being routed directly to physical Android smartphones[cite: 53].
* [cite_start]**Architecture Pending:** A concrete decision between a web-based or application-based platform is pending while hardware issues are resolved and the client finalizes their structural preference[cite: 54].
* [cite_start]**Project Feasibility:** The system requires an estimated 160 person-hours with a labor budget of R36,480[cite: 58, 59]. [cite_start]The project holds a projected payback period of 4–5 months based on anticipated savings from improved labor efficiency and reduced food waste[cite: 61].
